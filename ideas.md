<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Neumorphism (Soft UI)
  **Core Principles**:
  - Soft, extruded shapes that mimic physical buttons and cards
  - Low contrast shadows to create depth without harsh lines
  - Minimalist color palette to maintain focus on functionality
  - Tactile feel for interactive elements like buttons and toggles
  **Color Philosophy**:
  - Base: Light gray or off-white (#E0E5EC) to support shadow play
  - Accents: Soft blue or green for status indicators, keeping it calming
  - Text: Dark gray for readability, avoiding pure black to reduce strain
  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - Card-based layout where each element (student list, controls) feels like a physical object on a desk
  - Floating action buttons for primary actions
  **Signature Elements**:
  - Inner and outer shadows to define active/inactive states
  - Rounded corners (border-radius: 12px-20px) for a friendly, approachable look
  - Subtle gradients on backgrounds to enhance the 3D effect
  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - Buttons appear to press down when clicked (inset shadows)
  - Smooth transitions for state changes (e.g., switching between teacher/student view)
  **Animation**:
  - Gentle scaling on hover
  - Soft fade-ins for modal dialogs
  **Typography System**:
  - Font: Nunito or Quicksand (Rounded sans-serif)
  - Hierarchy: Bold headings with soft, readable body text
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Glassmorphism
  **Core Principles**:
  - Translucency and background blur to create a sense of hierarchy and depth
  - Vivid, colorful backgrounds that shine through frosted glass elements
  - Light borders to define edges of glass panels
  - Modern and sleek aesthetic
  **Color Philosophy**:
  - Background: Vibrant gradients (Purple/Blue/Pink mix) to emphasize the glass effect
  - Panels: White with low opacity and backdrop-blur
  - Text: White or very dark blue for contrast against glass
  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - Layered interface where modals and cards float above the background
  - Centralized dashboard with floating panels for different functions
  **Signature Elements**:
  - Backdrop-filter: blur(10px)
  - 1px solid white/transparent borders
  - Subtle noise texture to add realism to the glass
  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - Elements glow or become more opaque on hover
  - Fluid movement of background elements to create a dynamic feel
  **Animation**:
  - Parallax effects on background
  - Smooth sliding panels for navigation
  **Typography System**:
  - Font: Poppins or Montserrat (Geometric sans-serif)
  - Hierarchy: Clean, uppercase headings with widely spaced letters
</idea>

  **Design Movement**: Swiss Style (International Typographic Style)
  **Core Principles**:
  - Grid-based layout for mathematical precision
  - Asymmetric organization to create visual interest
  - Use of sans-serif typography as a primary design element
  - High contrast and clarity
  **Color Philosophy**:
  - Base: White or very light gray
  - Accents: Bold, primary colors (Red, Blue, Yellow) for status and actions
  - Text: Black for maximum legibility
  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - Strict modular grid
  - Clear separation of content areas using whitespace and lines
  **Signature Elements**:
  - Bold, large typography for numbers and headings
  - Minimalist icons
  - Strong horizontal and vertical lines
  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - Immediate, snappy feedback
  - Clear, unambiguous states for buttons and inputs
  **Animation**:
  - Fast, linear transitions
  - Simple geometric transformations
  **Typography System**:
  - Font: Helvetica Now or Inter (Neo-grotesque sans-serif)
  - Hierarchy: Large scale contrast between headings and body text
</idea>
